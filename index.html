I love you both!

To Moyo & Mutma

My absolute favorites

↓

To my two absolute favorites, the twin pillars of my sanity and the dual beats of my heart. To look at the two of you is to understand that soulmates aren't always romantic partners, sometimes, they are the women who hold your hand while you navigate the chaos of existence. You are my chosen family, a perfect balance of light and depth, sugar and spice, the storm and the shelter. A life without you two would be a book with blank pages, a song with no melody, a world completely devoid of color.

Moyo, my dazzling dancing queen, you are the spark that lights up every room and the gist that keeps my life entertaining. You feel so deeply, loving with your whole heart and wearing your emotions like the finest silk. I know you love the nice things in life, the luxury, the beauty, the shine, but the truth is, you are the nicest thing I know. I know you have a man who holds your heart, and he is a lucky guy, but he knows he has to share you with us. You are the fun, the rhythm, and the warmth that melts even the coldest days.

Mutma, my brilliant revolutionary, you are the anchor and the fire combined. Your mind is a library I never tire of exploring, a historian who knows where we’ve been and an activist fighting for where we need to go. You see the world not just as it is, but as it could be, and your intellect is as sharp as a blade. You love differently, freely, and outside the boxes the world tries to set, and that authenticity makes you radiant. In a world of noise, you are the signal, the truth teller, and the one who reminds me that power comes from within.

Together, you are the perfect symphony. One brings the party, the other brings the purpose, one brings the glitter, the other brings the grit. Moyo, you make sure we never forget to dance, and Mutma, you make sure we never forget to think. I am the luckiest person alive to stand between such softness and such strength, to be loved by the life of the party and the leader of the movement. You complete me in ways I didn't know were possible.

So, in the spirit of celebrating the greatest love story of my life, my friendship with you two, I have a question. Moyo, even though you’re taken, and Mutma, in all your rainbow glory, will you do me the honor?

Will you be my Galentines?

Will you let me spoil you both with the love, laughter, and appreciation you so richly deserve?

I Knew It! ❤️

I love you both!
